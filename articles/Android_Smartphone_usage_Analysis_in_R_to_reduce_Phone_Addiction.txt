Weekday vs Weeknd

This plot is to see if Iâ€™m really a phone addict even while at home with my family.

me %>%

filter(!str_detect(header,"com.")) %>%

group_by(Date = as.Date(time_ist)) %>%

ggplot(aes(fct_relevel(what_day, c("Weekday","Weekend")),
