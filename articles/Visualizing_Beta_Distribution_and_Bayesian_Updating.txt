Introduction

Beta distribution is a probability distribution of probabilities. What does it mean? Everyone started learning probability by flipping a coin. So letâ€™s start with a coin. First, define p as the probability of landing a head. If the coin is fair, then it is most likely that the coin will land head half of the time. In this case, p = 50% is the most likely value for p. But wait, it is also possible to have an unfair coin that behaves accidentally like a fair coin.
